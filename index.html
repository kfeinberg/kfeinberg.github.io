about me

junior @ brown university

Hi, my name is Kalissa! I’m a rising junior at Brown University pursuing a concentration in computer science. I am passionate about intuitive user experiences, technology as a means to improve society, and the role of technology design in our everyday lives. I previously attended Washington University in St. Louis where I served as Webmaster of the Women in Computer Science Club, teacher’s assistant in the courses Object Oriented Laboratory (CSE 332) and Data Structures and Algorithms (CSE 247), and as an Engineering tutor. Outside of computer science, I enjoy working on graphic design projects. Many of my pieces so far have focused on clean, thoughtful communication to a viewer, as well as rethinking literary pieces through visual means. Some of my hobbies include tennis, playing guitar, rock climbing, eating dark chocolate, and telling people that I am left-handed. On this website, you can find examples of both design and computer science projects I have worked on. Enjoy!
